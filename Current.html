Current



Currently studying towards my PhD at the Bristol Robotics Lab, I work with supervisor Jonathan Rossiter in the Soft Robotics group, investigating novel approaches to environmental and agricultural robotics. My vision for robotics in these fields are organic, earthy, wholesome robots, that live and die in the environment, the purpose of their life being to benefit both humans and the planet.

I am also working part time at the Knowle West Media Centre looking at ways to engage with the local community via a series of workshops. This will include creating a piece of robotic art for their new makerspace, The Factory. Find out more about our collaboration here.
