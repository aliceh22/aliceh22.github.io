With a logical mind and a creative heart, I am on an explorative journey to combine my passions for mathematics and art in interesting ways that benefit our world. I love to solve puzzles and am an enthusiastic co-creator, just name a project and lets go!

Based in Bristol, last summer I graduated from my Masters in Engineering Mathematics top of my year with First Class honours and a desire to continue modelling the world around me. During this time I also worked in research, finance and volunteered with over eight charities I care about deeply in areas such as conservation, education and accessibility of the arts. After a year of exploration, travel and learning how to build stuff I am now starting a phd at the Bristol Robotics Laboratory where I'll be getting down and dirty with soft robotics innovation and investigating the artistic, affective and dynamic potential of robotics.

I love to travel and explore, excited by finding new art and music - any excuse for a boogie! I'm learning french, salsa dancing, photography, kayaking and bouldering. If I could be out in nature all day I would be. To relax I sketch, paint, code and do yoga. I often play board games with friends although it has been said that I get too competitive...

That's enough about me, please explore the site to find out more and don't hesitate to get in touch!
