Raising Chickens

In lockdown 2020, my flatmate Elisa and I became inexplicably broody when talking about her parents chickens and decided to take our flatmate relationship to the next level. Her parents sent us some fertilised eggs in the post from their flock and we set about incubating them and preparing a run in the garden.

Incubating and hatching eggs turned out to be an emotional rollercoaster. We meticulously turned the eggs 3 times everyday and made sure the temperature and humidity round the eggs was just right. We made inspirational posters for them to encourage only female chicks to hatch since roosters are unfortunately not appropriate for inner-city living. Of 8 eggs we had 4 chicks hatch on their own and 1 chick that had to have some help
