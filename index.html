Hello

My name is Alice. I am a maker and roboticist from Bristol, UK, currently based in Saarbrücken, Germany.

At the core of my pursuits is a fascination with interfaces - the boundaries between us, within us and around us. How we interface with technology, other beings and ourselves. On a social level, these interfaces define our ability to communicate across space and time. They are becoming increasingly less and less tangible and more and more virtual, but I like to bring things back to earth and back to our physical bodies. My work (and play) currently revolve around the following themes...

> community <
People-oriented practices, workshops and projects.

> research <
Investigating how human interactions can be mediated by technology.

> craft <
Making, designing and playing, using both traditional and modern techniques.


Please note: This website is not updated regularly and I am not currently on social media - this is due to re-evaluating what internet presence I would like to have and prioritising more tangible interactions. Please get in touch via email (or letter even!) if you would like to find out more about my current work or discuss collaborations. I'd love to hear from you.
